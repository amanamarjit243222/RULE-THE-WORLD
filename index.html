Command Directives (How to Play)

1. Objective Maintain power, grow your economy, and project global dominance. If your Overall Approval drops to 0%, your Treasury hits bankruptcy, or Global Tension reaches 100% (nuclear war), you lose.
2. Economy & Demographics Manage taxation, military, and R&D spending to maintain positive Net Income. Your policies affect Youth, Workers, Rural, and Elites differently.
3. Political Capital (CP) CP generates over time. Use it to enact National Policies (right panel) and resolve Global Crises. Keep reserves for sudden emergencies!
4. Warfare & Tension Click foreign nations to initiate wars. Warfare requires upkeep and CP. Winning grants buffs, losing damages approval. Note: Wars drastically increase Global Tension.
5. System Crises Random global crises will periodically emerge. Solve them using CP and Treasury funds before they severely drain your economy and public support.
