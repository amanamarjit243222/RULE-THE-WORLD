RULE THE WORLD

Become a Leader

A hyperrealistic geopolitical & macroeconomic browser strategy simulator set in 2029. Choose from 7 world powers, wage multi-domain warfare, and achieve global dominance.

Why Play RULE THE WORLD?

Seven playable factions · Nineteen policy actions · Unlimited strategic choices · Zero pay-to-win

🌐

Live D3 World Map

Interact with a fully rendered high-resolution world map powered by D3.js. Click any nation to view intelligence, select targets, or broker deals. Zoomable, pannable, and colour-coded by alignment, stability, or GDP growth.

📊

Realistic Macroeconomics

Model real 2029 GDP projections, inflation spirals, debt interest compounding, and recession cycles. Balance four demographic approval groups — Youth, Working Class, Rural, and Elites — as policies ripple through society.

⚔

Multi-Domain Warfare

Launch Military strikes, Trade sanctions, Cyber offensives, or wage Cold War influence campaigns. Each conflict type has unique upkeep costs, risk profiles, and power-roll outcomes. Animated units fly across the map in real-time.

🤝

Dynamic Diplomacy

Track bilateral relations with 6 AI-powered rival and allied factions. Relations drift naturally. AI factions request aid, form alliances, and declare war based on your tension level and their strategic interests.

🚨

Cascading Global Crises

Nine unique system crises — from supply chain collapses to pandemic outbreaks — can occur simultaneously and cascade. Unresolved crises drain your GDP and approval. Up to three concurrent crises force brutal prioritisation.

🏆

Five Victory Paths

Win via World Domination (15+ territories), Eternal Leadership (40 yrs at 75% approval), Tech Supremacy (Tech Level 3.0), Peacemaker (8 yrs near-zero tension), or Diplomatic Grandmaster (full alliances for 20 years).

Command Directives

1. ObjectiveMaintain power, grow your economy, and project global dominance. Approval at 0%, treasury at -$3T, or tension at 100% = game over.
2. EconomyManage taxation, military, R&D, and welfare to maintain positive Net Income. Higher tax squeezes elites. Higher welfare boosts workers. Deficits accrue interest.
3. Political Capital (CP)CP accumulates over time. Spend it on National Policies and resolving crises. Crises can now cascade — prioritise resolution.
4. Warfare & Tension (NEW: Harder)Wars now cost more upkeep. Tension ≥ 80% forces AI aggression. Victory requires 15 territories (up from 10). Ceasefire is harder to achieve.
5. Victory ConditionsWorld Dominator (15 nations), Eternal Leader (40 yrs, 75% approval), Tech Supremacy (Tech 3.0 + 5 territories), Peacemaker (8 yrs tension ≤ 3%), Diplomatic Grandmaster (all relations ≥ 70 for 20 yrs).

About This Game

RULE THE WORLD: Become a Leader is a free, browser-based geopolitical macro-strategy simulator. It is entirely fictional and created for entertainment and educational purposes only.

Gameplay: Players choose from 7 world powers (India, USA, China, Russia, EU, Brazil, Nigeria) and must manage economics, demographics, foreign policy, and military strategy to achieve one of five victory conditions across a simulated 2029-era global geopolitical landscape.

Technology: Built with D3.js (interactive world map), TopoJSON (border data), Firebase (cloud saves), and Web Audio API (procedural ambient music). No plugins or app downloads required — runs entirely in your browser.

Disclaimer: This is a work of fiction. All economic figures, political alignments, and scenarios are invented for the purpose of gameplay. No real geopolitical statements or positions are intended or should be inferred.
